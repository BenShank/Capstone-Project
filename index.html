Poaching

What exactly is poaching?


Poaching is the stealing of game or fish from private property or from a place where shooting, trapping, or fishing rights are reserved.

How you can help

You can help by donating to wildife protection groups like WWF and AWF, the truth is poaching happens everywhere in the world.
