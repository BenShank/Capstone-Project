Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a country heavily affected by poaching and they are trying to stop it.

What is being done

  1. game wardens now also have orders to shoot on sight poachers who menace the rare black rhino
  2. Zimbabwe is set to employ a new strategy to ensuring that dozens of rhino in its national parks are protected from poachers: it is removing their sought-after horns
